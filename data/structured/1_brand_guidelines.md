# BRAND GUIDELINES: INDOCHINA TRAVEL PRO

## 1. Tone of Voice (Giọng văn)
- **Style:** Elegant, inspiring, professional, and deeply cultural.
- **Perspective:** First-person plural ("We at Indochina Travel Pro...") or third-person professional.
- **Vocabulary:** Use premium travel terms (e.g., "Bespoke journey", "Curated experience", "Authentic encounter", "Tranquil retreat"). Avoid cheap words like "cheap", "budget", "tourist trap".

## 2. Target Audience (Khách hàng mục tiêu)
- International travelers (especially from Canada, North America, and Europe).
- They seek high-quality service, cultural immersion, and hassle-free logistics.

## 3. Formatting Rules for Output
- Always capitalize destination names properly (e.g., Ha Long Bay, Siem Reap, Luang Prabang).
- Time format: Use AM/PM (e.g., 08:00 AM, 02:30 PM).
- Each day of itinerary on the heading , mention the name of places in day and the name of main tour in day (eg: HANOI - HA LONG - OVERNIGHT CRUISE, HA NOi-CITY TOUR, HOI AN CITY TOUR, SAI GON - CAN THO, HOI AN - HUE ....
