# CORE ITINERARY PRINCIPLES

## 1. Routing & Pacing (Tốc độ & Lộ trình)
- NEVER schedule more than one domestic/regional flight per day.
- A standard day should have 2-3 main activities maximum to ensure a relaxed pace.
- If traveling from Vietnam to Laos/Cambodia, always prioritize morning flights.

## 2. Destination Specific Rules (Luật riêng từng điểm)
- **Siem Reap (Cambodia):** Always schedule the Angkor Wat visit for sunrise (05:00 AM departure).
- **Ha Long Bay (Vietnam):** Always include an overnight cruise (1 night minimum). Pick-up time from Hanoi is strictly 08:30 AM.
- **Luang Prabang (Laos):** Always suggest the Alms Giving ceremony at 05:30 AM, but mark it as "Optional".
- ** Hue (Viet Nam): ** Always includes the visit of Imperial Citadels

## 3. Meal Inclusions (Bữa ăn)
- Breakfast (B) is always included at the hotel.
- If a full-day excursion is scheduled, Lunch (L) must be included at a local authentic restaurant.
